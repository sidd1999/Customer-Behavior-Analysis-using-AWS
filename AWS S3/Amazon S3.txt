Amazon S3 is used to store the raw and processed data.

Steps used to load data:
Click on Create bucket.
Provide a unique name for your bucket.
Select the region where you want the bucket to be located.
Leave most of the default settings, but you can enable versioning or encryption based on your requirements.

Once the bucket is created, click on the bucket name to open it.
Click "Upload," then "Add files."
Select the file to upload.
Click "Upload" to store the file in the S3 bucket.
