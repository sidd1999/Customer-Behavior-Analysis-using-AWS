Capture and stream the real-time clickstream data.

Steps:
-Click on "Create data stream" to start the process.
-A shard is a unit of capacity in Kinesis. Each shard can handle up to 1MB of incoming data per second and 2MB of outgoing data per second.
-After configuring the settings, click on "Create data stream."
-Once the stream is active, it will be listed on your Kinesis dashboard.
